About Us

In this group you are going to meet fellow drone enthusiasts and this will be the place where the knoweldge is share for everyone.
Amongst other things one can find help from our moderators regarding anything you get stuck on, drone techincal assistance,
recomendations of drones, and any questions in relation to laws and permits one might need.

For more information on laws and permits scroll down below.

Laws

To start making use of your drone, the first thing you are going to need a permit and permision from the proper authorities.
We reccomend you making a visit to Civil Aviation Directorate to Register your drone type and make sure you have the training to fully operate your drone.
Once the drone is registered and insured properly, you can pick a location of where you can lawfully fly the drone and a permit will be issued for that time,
and you can start flying your drone whilst obeying the law.
